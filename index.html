About me

I have had serious passion for art and drawing from a very young age, in 2010, I started looking for ways I could draw digitally, and I tried my hands on apps like Gimp, and manga studio. I eventually stuck with Photoshop. Although I have always loved art, I never really saw it as something that could be monetized, neither did I see the need take its practice seriously, especially during my college days. I always felt too busy to actually draw and that mindset really slowed down my art progress up until late 2017.

I am currently a freelance digital artist and illustrator, and I have taken it upon myself to at least do something art related every single day, weather it is a complete painting, a sketch, or just simple lines the goal is consistency.
